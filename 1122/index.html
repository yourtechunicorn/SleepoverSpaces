01 — Arrival
Checking In
At the Lobby
Present to Guard
1
Approach the front desk and say you have a reservation with Sleepover Spaces.Say: "I'm checking in with Sleepover Spaces, Unit —."
2
Present a valid government-issued ID — passport, driver's license, or national ID. One per adult guest.
3
Fill the visitor logbook if asked — name, unit number, duration of stay.
4
Head to the service elevator at the back near the convenience store — it's the closest one to Unit 1122. Your door has a keypad lock — tap the front with your palm, enter your code, then press ✱.
